\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\subsubsection*{template$<$typename T, typename Comparator$>$\newline
class Tree\+::binary\+Tree$<$ T, Comparator $>$}

用二叉链表实现的二叉树类 


\begin{DoxyTemplParams}{Template Parameters}
{\em T} & 数据类型 \\
\hline
{\em Comparator} & 数据类型的比较器类，默认为std\+::less$<$\+T$>$\\
\hline
\end{DoxyTemplParams}
